Exploring Imagination with Creativity.

About us

We transform your imagination to reality with our incredible range of wooden toys and products.

What We Do
We, at Mebtoys, promise 'A Jolly Good Time!' with our carefully crafted toys and products. Combined with our equipment and skilled master crafters, we manufacture around 2000 toys per day. We put an incredible amount of thought into each of our products as we want them to be a companion in your creative journey and also act as a knowledge stimulator and practical science assistant. We believe actions make a brand and we contribute in our own small ways to make the world a better place. We ensure that 10% of all our profits proceed to various charitable institutions across India. We have 25 physically challenged families working with us. Mebtoys is proud to be a participant of regular product donation drive to various NGOs and orphanages across India.
Who We Are
Mebtoys is a culmination of creativity and imagination. We believe that true joy is found where the creativity of a craft meets the unbounded extent of a child’s imagination.
It opens one’s mind to a new wave of inspiration. We have always had a yearning to make an impact. Not only with our products, but with our contributions to the society. Mebtoys was established in 2013.We are spread over 2,10,000 sq.ft. of lush green land with a dedicated workshop spanning over an impressive 43,000 sq.ft of ﬂoor space.We are readily equipped with machines and units like a treating plant, paint houses, design sections, SMG areas, to name a few. We have a crack team of highly experienced designers and over 27 years of design experience under our belt.
How We Work
We take product quality and ecological needs very seriously.

From our products being made out of the best sustainable wood, all the way up to using natural paints, we want our products to be considered as a benchmark in the industry. Made as per EU and US Standards, our products are economical and safe. Wooden toys and products stand the test of time due to their durability. They are bio-degradable and non toxic. We want out products to be passed down to younger generations in the family as a valued heritage.
What We Believe
We are on a mission - Fun, Learning and Smiles.
​
Our mission, day in and day out, is to consistently develop and manufacture high quality toys and products for children that are a delight to hold, play with and as a companion.
We want our products to be a source of immeasurable joy, prove as an educational source by providing practical knowledge to everyone and also be a polished product worthy of being passed down the family as a heritage.
