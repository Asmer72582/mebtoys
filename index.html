Who We Are

Mebtoys is a culmination of creativity and imagination. We believe that true joy is found where the creativity of a craft meets the unbounded extent of a child’s imagination. It opens one’s mind to a new wave of inspiration. We have always had a yearning to make an impact. Not only with our products, but with our contributions to the society. Mebtoys was established in 2013.We are spread over 2,10,000 sq.ft. of lush green land with a dedicated workshop spanning over an impressive 43,000 sq.ft of ﬂoor space.We are readily equipped with machines and units like a treating plant, paint houses, design sections, SMG areas, to name a few. We have a crack team of highly experienced designers and over 27 years of design experience under our belt.

How We Work ​

We take product quality and ecological needs very seriously. From our products being made out of the best sustainable wood, all the way up to using natural paints, we want our products to be considered as a benchmark in the industry. Made as per EU and US Standards, our products are economical and safe. Wooden toys and products stand the test of time due to their durability. They are bio-degradable and non toxic. We want out products to be passed down to younger generations in the family as a valued heritage.
