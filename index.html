Welcome to Mebtoys

Crafting customized wooden toys, wooden educational tools, and fun-oriented wooden playthings for imaginative minds.

WHAT WE DO

Mebtoys guarantees joy with 2000 daily crafted toys, fostering creativity and practical learning. Committed to social impact, 10% of profits aid charitable causes, proudly supporting 25 physically challenged families.

WHO WE ARE

Mebtoys, crafting joy since 2013 with creativity and imagination. Our 2,10,000 sq.ft. facility and 27 years of design experience drive impactful contributions to both products and society.

HOW WE WORK​

Crafted from sustainable wood, meeting EU and US Standards, our products prioritize quality and ecology. Economical, safe, and designed to last, they're meant to be cherished family heirlooms.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Our mission is simple yet profound: Fun, Learning, and Smiles. We dedicate ourselves to crafting high-quality toys that bring joy, impart practical knowledge, and stand as polished heirlooms for generations.
